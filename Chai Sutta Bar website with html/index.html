About Us

Why We Rock?

The Vision of Chai Sutta Bar is to deliver our unique culture blend and values to each corner of the world. Slowly people started knowing them and their shop name Chai Sutta bar, withing 6month they are doing very good sales and need 5-6 people just to handle the crowd. They started working on expanding and opening new franchises and now they have more than 165 outlets, not only in India but also in Dubai, Muscat, Oman, Nepal. Apart from this, outlets are also going to open in Canada and America.
